Espirito AF Cup

     Espirito
  26, 28 January 2018
   Prize worth 70k
   7:00 AM Ownwards

Description

Espirito AF Cup is the second edition of Ace Football Cup. It will be a 5-a-side
League + Knockout tournament where every team will get 2 matches to play. The top teams in the league phase will qualify to the knockout rounds.

Registration fees : 3000 Rs/Team

Rules

  1. Each team should have a minimum of 8 players and maximum of 10 players.
  2. You are only permitted to have five substitutes -unlimited interchange (Only when game is stopped)
  3. Tackles from behind and slide tackles are not allowed.
  4. Every team should complete the registration process before the specified dates to confirm entry into the tournament
  5. A team is allowed 1 free team member replacement before the knockout rounds.
  6. Registration fees once paid will not be refunded under any circumstance.
  7. Abusive language to the referee or any player is not acceptable, The referee will stop play if any abusive language is heard.
  8. Any walkover or disqualification is considered as 3-0 win for the opposition.
  9. It is compulsory for all players to WEAR SHIN PADS.
  10. All players must follow the referees instructions and decisions throughout the game.
  11. If a player takes a penalty, they can only take one step.
  12. Standard Futsal rules apply.
